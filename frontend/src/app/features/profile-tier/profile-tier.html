How ELO works

Every ranked match adjusts your ELO based on your opponent's rating and the result. Beat a higher-rated player and you gain more; lose to a lower-rated one and you lose more. Climb through the tiers by playing consistently and answering quickly.

Solo, Duel, and Logo Quiz each track separate ELO. The rank shown here is your overall solo ELO.
