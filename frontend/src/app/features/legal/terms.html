2. Description of Service

StepOver is a football (soccer) trivia quiz application offering multiple game modes, including:

  • Solo Mode — single-player ELO-rated quiz sessions against the system
  • Logo Quiz — identify football clubs from modified versions of their logos
  • Daily Challenge — one curated quiz available each day
  • Multiplayer / Online Game — real-time competitive matches against other players
  • Duel Mode — head-to-head one-on-one challenges
  • Battle Royale — multi-player elimination format where wrong answers eliminate players
  • 2-Player Mode — local same-device multiplayer quiz

Additional game modes may be added over time. We may also modify or retire existing modes at our discretion.

Quiz questions are generated with the assistance of AI and may cover all aspects of football including players, clubs, competitions, history, and statistics. We do not guarantee the accuracy of every question and answer, though we make reasonable efforts to ensure quality.
