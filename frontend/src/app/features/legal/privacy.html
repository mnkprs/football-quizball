Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Stepover ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Stepover application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over it.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and processing your data lawfully, transparently, and fairly. By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at support@stepover.app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Data

When you create an account — either via Google OAuth or email/password — we collect and store:

  • Email address
  • Display name / username (chosen by you)
  • Profile avatar (if provided via Google or uploaded by you)
  • Account creation date and last login date

If you sign in with Google, we receive only the email address and name that Google shares with us at sign-in. We do not receive your Google password.

Game Data

As you play, we record the following to power your stats and leaderboards:

  • ELO rating and ELO history (changes per game)
  • Number of games played, questions answered, and correct answers
  • Game mode participation and results
  • Match outcomes in multiplayer, duel, and battle royale modes
  • Daily challenge completion status

Usage and Analytics Data

We use PostHog analytics to understand how users interact with the Service. This may include:

  • Pages and screens visited within the App
  • Game modes accessed and session duration
  • Button clicks and feature interactions
  • Approximate geographic region (country level, derived from IP)
  • Referring URL (if accessed via web)

PostHog analytics data is collected only with your consent. You can opt out via the cookie consent banner.

Payment Data

Pro subscriptions are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card number, CVC, or bank account details. We store only:

  • Stripe Customer ID — a reference token used to manage your subscription
  • Subscription status (active, cancelled, past due)
  • Billing period dates

Device and Technical Data

When you use the App, your browser or device may automatically transmit:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Screen resolution
  • IP address (used for security and fraud prevention; not stored long-term)
  • Time zone

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver the game service, including matchmaking, question generation, and leaderboards
  • Track and display your quiz statistics and ELO progress
  • Process Pro subscription payments via Stripe
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, subscription receipts)
  • Improve the App based on aggregated usage analytics
  • Detect and prevent fraud, cheating, or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use it for profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar local storage mechanisms. These fall into the following categories:

Essential Cookies

Required for the Service to function. These cannot be disabled:

  • Authentication session — keeps you logged in (managed by Supabase)
  • Theme preference — remembers your dark/light mode choice
  • Cookie consent choice — stores whether you accepted or declined non-essential cookies
  • Onboarding status — remembers if you have completed the onboarding flow

Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

  • PostHog — product analytics to understand feature usage and improve the App

Advertising Cookies (Consent Required)

  • Google AdSense — used to serve personalised advertisements to Free tier users. The AdSense script is only loaded after you explicitly accept cookies via the consent banner. Pro subscribers do not see ads.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage, which will prompt the consent banner again on your next visit.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase, a cloud database platform built on PostgreSQL. Data is hosted on servers located within the European Union (AWS eu-west-1 region). All data in transit between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS). Data at rest is encrypted by Supabase at the storage level.

6. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google LLC — Sign In with Google (OAuth identity provider); Google AdSense (advertising, consent-gated). Google Privacy Policy
  • Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing for Pro subscriptions. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • PostHog, Inc. — Product analytics (consent-gated). PostHog Privacy Policy
  • Supabase, Inc. — Database, authentication, and real-time infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Vercel, Inc. — Frontend hosting and edge delivery. Vercel Privacy Policy

We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to function. We do not permit third parties to use your data for their own marketing or advertising purposes unless you have separately consented to that (e.g., via Google's own ad personalisation settings).

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account and game data: retained until you delete your account
  • ELO history: retained as part of your account record; deleted on account deletion
  • Analytics data: retained per PostHog's default retention policy (typically up to 12 months)
  • Payment records: Stripe retains transaction records for up to 7 years for legal and tax compliance

When you delete your account, your profile and all associated game data are permanently deleted from our database via a cascade delete. Some anonymised aggregate statistics (e.g., total questions answered globally) may be retained.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with equivalent data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — You can view your profile data, game statistics, and ELO history directly in the App under Profile settings.
  • Right to Data Portability — You can request an export of your personal data by emailing support@stepover.app.
  • Right to Erasure — You can delete your account at any time from the Profile settings page. This will permanently remove your data from our systems.
  • Right to Rectification — You can update your username and profile details within the App. For other corrections, contact us at support@stepover.app.
  • Right to Object — You can object to non-essential cookies and analytics via the cookie consent banner. You can opt out of Google ad personalisation via Google's own settings.
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Contact us to request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@stepover.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, or your national DPA in the EU).

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@stepover.app and we will promptly delete that data.

Users between 13 and 16 in the EU may require parental consent under GDPR Article 8. We rely on parents and guardians to supervise minors' use of the Service.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS)
  • Database access is restricted to authenticated service roles with row-level security (RLS) policies
  • Authentication tokens are managed by Supabase and are short-lived
  • Production secrets are stored in environment variables, not in source code
  • We do not store passwords in plaintext; authentication is handled via Supabase Auth or Google OAuth

No system is completely secure. If you discover a security vulnerability in the Service, please report it responsibly to support@stepover.app.
