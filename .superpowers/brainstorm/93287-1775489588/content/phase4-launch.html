App Preview Video

What: 15-30 second preview video for the App Store listing
Flow: Open app → tap Solo → answer a question (correct, haptic flash) → ELO goes up → swipe to Duel → matchmaking → cut to Battle Royale lobby filling up → end on profile with achievements + streak flame
Audio: Upbeat background music (royalty-free), UI sounds from Phase 1
Specs: iOS: 1080x1920 or 1920x1080, H.264, 15-30s. Google Play: up to 30s, landscape or portrait.
Scope: Screen recording + light editing. Not a produced ad — just real gameplay.
