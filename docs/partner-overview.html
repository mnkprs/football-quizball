⚽ What is QuizBall?

QuizBall is a web-based football trivia platform that lets fans compete, learn, and engage with the sport they love every single day. Players can challenge each other locally, race the clock in a solo sprint, climb a global ranking ladder, or tackle a fresh daily puzzle — all powered by artificial intelligence that generates and validates every question automatically.

The platform requires no downloads and works on any device. Users log in with their Google account and immediately have access to a personalised experience with stats, rankings, and history tracked over time.

Core idea: football knowledge meets competitive gaming. Every session is different because the questions are generated fresh by AI — there is no static question bank to exhaust.

🎮 Game Modes

QuizBall offers four distinct ways to play, each designed for a different occasion and audience.

🏆
Solo Ranked — The Climbing Ladder
Login required
A head-to-head between the player and the AI. Each question is generated on the spot at a difficulty level matched to the player's current skill rating. Answer correctly to gain points; answer wrong or run out of time to lose them. The difficulty adapts automatically — beginners get straightforward questions, advanced players face obscure and niche topics. A full ranking ladder (leaderboard) shows where every player sits globally.
⚡
Blitz — 60 Seconds on the Clock
Login required
A pure speed challenge. The player has 60 seconds to answer as many multiple-choice football questions as possible. Each question flashes by with a one-second result before the next one appears. The session ends when time runs out, and the final score is saved to the Blitz leaderboard. Players can always try to beat their personal best.
📅
Today in Football — The Daily Habit
No login needed
Every day at midnight the AI generates a fresh set of 8 questions based on real football events that happened on that calendar date in history — transfers, match results, trophies, retirements, records. All players worldwide see the same questions on the same day. It is a quick, social format built to bring users back every day.
👥
2-Player — Local Head-to-Head
No login needed
Two players on the same device compete on a quiz board with 8 categories and 3 difficulty levels per category — 24 questions in total. Categories include History, Player ID, Guess the Score, Higher or Lower, Top 5, Geography, Gossip, and News. Players take turns choosing their questions, earn points per correct answer, and can use special power-ups (50/50 lifeline, 2× score multiplier). Answers are typed freely and validated by the AI, which understands approximate and informal responses.

🤖 How AI Powers the Platform

The artificial intelligence engine is at the core of what makes QuizBall different from any static trivia app. Rather than pulling from a fixed database of questions, QuizBall uses a large language model (Google's Gemini) to generate, validate, and score every question dynamically.

Question Generation

For each game session, the AI creates brand-new questions based on the mode and the player's profile. A Solo question is tailored to the player's exact skill level — the AI knows whether to ask about well-known Champions League winners or obscure 1980s transfer records. A Daily question is grounded in real historical football events on that exact date. A 2-Player board contains 24 questions spanning 8 different topic areas, all created on the fly.

Answer Validation

In modes where players type free-text answers (Solo and 2-Player), the AI evaluates whether the response is correct — not just exact character matching. This means "Ronaldo", "C. Ronaldo", and "Cristiano" can all be accepted for the same answer. The AI handles abbreviations, spelling variations, and common nicknames, creating a much fairer and more natural experience.

Adaptive Difficulty

In Solo Ranked mode, every player has a skill rating (ELO score, the same system used in chess). The AI generates questions at the right difficulty for each player's current level. As a player improves, the questions automatically become harder. This ensures the game always feels challenging without being unfair.

Translation & Localisation

The platform currently supports English and Greek. The AI translates questions and explanations into Greek in real time, preserving proper nouns (player and club names) while adapting the rest of the text naturally. The language switch is available on every screen with a single tap.

🚀 The User Journey

First Visit (No Account)

A new visitor lands on the home screen and can immediately start a 2-Player local game or take the Daily Quiz — no registration required. This lowers the barrier to entry and lets users experience the product before committing to sign up.

Sign Up

Signing up takes one click via Google. The system automatically creates a profile with a starting ELO of 1,000. From that point, every answer in Solo and Blitz modes is tracked and contributes to the player's long-term rating and history.

Regular Play Loop

📅
Daily Check-In
Open the app, play the 8 daily questions in under 3 minutes. Same time every day builds a habit. No account needed — perfect for casual fans.
🏆
Ranking Grind
Log in and play Solo Ranked to climb the leaderboard. ELO goes up or down after each question, creating tension and motivation to keep playing.
⚡
Speed Run
Jump into Blitz for a quick 60-second session. Beat your personal best. Check the leaderboard. Try again. Fast and addictive.
👥
Social Play
Challenge a friend or family member on the same screen. No account needed. Works at watch parties, bars, or football events.

⚙️ Under the Hood (Brief)

QuizBall is a fully cloud-based product. There is nothing to install — users access it through any web browser. The platform is built on modern, scalable technology and is designed to handle growth without major re-architecture.

Angular 20 (Web App) NestJS (Backend API) Supabase (Database) Google Gemini AI Google OAuth (Login) Railway (Hosting) English + Greek Mobile Responsive

Deployment: The backend and frontend are independently deployable and currently hosted on Railway (backend) and Vercel (frontend). The database is managed by Supabase. All infrastructure is cloud-native with no physical servers to maintain.
